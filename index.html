About Kraese Repairs

I offer parts and repair services for all major brand of outdoor power equipment such as lawn mowers, snowblowers, handheld power equipment, go karts, etc. I have years of experience working with small engine machinery and repair leading to unparalleled expertise in the area.


Our Services

We offer a variety of service options to keep our customers as happy as possible.

Small Engine Service

Tune up your engine to keep it running for years. This service will elongate the lifetime of your equipment and save hundreds of dollars.

Small Engine Repair

Having issues with your equipment? Bring it by for a quick diagnosis, and effective repair service to eliminate all those squeaks and rattles.

Oil Sales

We carry a full line of top notch oils and dependable fuels for small engine equipment to keep optimal performance for your euipment.

Engine Repowering
